Reupholster your sofa

By repairing & reupholstering (or recovering) furniture, you can maintain the integrity of your beloved items and enjoy them for many many years to come.

That is where Balu's Home.com can help!

How much to reupholster a sofa?? Depends on the suite, material & current condition!

Repair & Re-upholstery is a cost effective option to restore your furniture at a fraction of the replacement cost.

Reupholstery Items Of Furniture

We can repair and reupholstery any item of furniture, be it leather or fabric, old or new, including;

    • Sofa
    • Couch
    • Office furniture
    • Dining chairs
    • Kitchen chairs

Furniture Repair Services

We offer a range of furniture repair service for both leather and fabric, including;

    • Framework
    • Springs
    • Tears
    • Replacement Panels in fabric or leather
    • Replacement cushions
    • Replacement foam

We guide you through the whole re-upholstery process

    • Contact us and we can discuss your requirements. Include information on the size of the item to be reupholstered. Ideally Send us a picture of the item.
    • Let us know which Fabric you would like it to be upholstered in. We have a large selection and it would be good to visit our display so you pick a fabric to your taste.
    • We will provide you with a free quotation detailing the price including the material(fabric/leather).
    • Once agreed, we can arrange to collect the furniture and start the reupholstery.
    • Get your new reupholstered furniture in around 2-3 weeks!
